Upstream threats

Legal pressure

California's AB 1043 was signed into law and takes effect January 1, 2027. It requires operating systems to provide real time age bracket APIs. Colorado's SB 26-051 follows the same model. Brazil's Lei 15.211 is already enacted. Illinois SB3977 is in progress. These laws are why the upstream projects above are building age verification infrastructure.
